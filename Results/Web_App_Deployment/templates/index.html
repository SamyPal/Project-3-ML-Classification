Coronary Heart Disease (CHD) Risk Calculator

Every year, about 805,000 Americans have a heart attack. This app determines a persons risk for contracting CHD, based on their physical health. The app uses a Random Forest machine learning model, trained with the data from Framingham heart study. The model is biased towards the demographics of Framingham.
