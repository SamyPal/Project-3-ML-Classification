A Simple App to Evaluate the Heart Risk to Contract a Coronary Heart Disease (CHD)

Every year, about 805,000 Americans have a heart attack. This is simple flask app that determines a persons risk for contracting CHD, based on their physical health.
The app uses a Random Forest machine learning model, trained with the data from Framingham heart study.
The model, as the data, is biased towards the demographics of Framingham, at the time of the study.
